about us

In the heart of Gulbahar, Peshawar, you'll find PMS English Language Class. Our main goal is to help students learn English really well. We want to give people the skills to talk confidently, not only in their local community but also with people all around the world. We also want to make learning language fun and exciting. Our classes are like a journey where you discover the power of words and the joy of understanding. We welcome you to join us on this adventure of learning and exploring!

Qualifications that Shine Bright:

With a wealth of educational accomplishments under her belt, our English teacher's qualifications are nothing short of remarkable. Her journey is adorned with accolades, including a successful conquest of the IELTS test and a collection of English diplomas that showcase her commitment to continuous learning and mastery.

Teaching Methodology that Ignites Minds:

What truly sets our English teacher apart is her distinctive approach to teaching. Gone are the days of monotony and drudgery; her classes are an engaging tapestry of creativity, interaction, and real-world applicability. Individualized Learning, Lasting Impact:

In her classroom, every student is a unique star on their own journey. Our teacher recognizes that each student has their pace and learning style. With a nurturing spirit, she takes the time to understand and cater to the individual needs of every learner. No question too small, and no challenge is insurmountable under her guidance.

But the impact of our English teacher stretches far beyond language acquisition. Her classroom is a microcosm of connections, friendships, and personal growth. In conclusion, our English teacher isn't just an educator; she is a catalyst for transformation. Her qualifications shine as a testament to her expertise, but it's her teaching methodology and the heart she invests in her students that truly make her exceptional. With her, learning English isn't just a requirement; it's a lifelong journey of exploration and enlightenment. Q2: Who can join the English classes at PMS?

Our classes are open to everyone! Whether you're a student, a working professional, or someone who wants to improve their English skills, you're welcome to join us.

Q3: What teaching methods do you use to help students learn English?

We use interactive and engaging teaching methods. Our classes involve conversations, activities, and real-life examples to make learning English enjoyable and effective.

Q4: Do you offer different levels of English classes?

Yes, we offer classes for different levels, from beginners to more advanced learners. This way, you can start at the level that matches your current skills.

Q5: Can I visit your center to learn more about the classes before joining?

Absolutely! We encourage you to visit us. You can meet our team, see our facilities, and ask any questions you might have. Just let us know when you'd like to visit, and we'll be happy to show you around.
